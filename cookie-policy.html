1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the owners of the site.

Cookies can be categorized in different ways:

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
  • First-party cookies: Set by the website you are visiting
  • Third-party cookies: Set by a third party (such as analytics providers)

2. Cookies This Site Uses

JW Images Search uses only essential localStorage (functionally equivalent to cookies) to remember your preferences and provide core functionality. We do not use any tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies.

3. Third-Party Services

This site uses the Google Custom Search API to provide search functionality. When you perform a search:

  • Your search query is sent to our serverless function (hosted on Vercel)
  • Our serverless function makes a server-to-server request to Google's API
  • Google processes the request and returns search results to our server
  • Our server forwards only the search results to your browser

Important: No Google tracking cookies are set on your device by this app. The Google API call is made server-to-server, and your browser only communicates with our serverless function.

4. How to Manage or Withdraw Consent

You have full control over your cookie preferences:

Through the Cookie Banner

When you first visit the site, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all: Consents to the use of essential localStorage
  • Reject non-essential: Since we only use essential localStorage, this has the same functional effect as "Accept all"

Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw your consent at any time by clicking the button below. This will clear your cookie consent preference and restore the consent banner on your next visit.

Browser Settings

You can also manage or delete localStorage data through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
