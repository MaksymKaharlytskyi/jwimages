1. Identity and Contact Details of the Data Controller

For the purposes of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws, the data controller for JW Images Search is:

Data Controller: JW Images Search

2. What Personal Data Is Processed

JW Images Search does not collect, store, or process any personal data.

Specifically:

  • No personal data is collected or stored server-side
  • No user accounts or registration is required
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising cookies or pixels are employed
  • No personal data is shared with third parties

Local Storage Only

The only data stored by this application is in your browser's localStorage, which resides exclusively on your device. This includes:

  • cookie_consent: Your cookie consent preference (stores "accepted" or nothing)
  • last_query: Your last search query for convenience (non-personal)

This localStorage data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time through your browser settings
  • Does not identify you as an individual

Search Queries

When you perform a search:

  • Your search query is sent to our serverless function (hosted on Vercel)
  • The query is processed and immediately forwarded to Google's Custom Search API
  • No search queries are logged, stored, or retained by JW Images Search
  • Vercel (our hosting provider) may temporarily log request data as part of normal operations, subject to their privacy policy

3. Purpose and Legal Basis for Processing

Since JW Images Search does not collect or process personal data, the GDPR's legal bases for processing do not apply in the traditional sense.

For the minimal localStorage data stored on your device:

  • Purpose: Essential functionality (remembering your consent choice and last search)
  • Legal Basis: Legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR) - providing basic functionality that users expect

4. Data Retention

Server-side: No personal data is retained server-side.

Local storage: Data stored in your browser's localStorage persists until:

  • You clear your browser's data
  • You withdraw consent through the cookie policy page
  • You use your browser's private/incognito mode (data is deleted when you close the session)

5. Data Subject Rights

Under the GDPR, individuals have certain rights regarding their personal data. Since JW Images Search does not collect or process personal data, most of these rights are not applicable. However, for completeness:

Right of Access (Article 15)

You have the right to request confirmation as to whether personal data concerning you is being processed. Since we do not process personal data, there is no data to provide.

Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You have the right to have inaccurate personal data rectified. You can clear or modify any localStorage data through your browser settings.

Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data. You can clear your browser's localStorage at any time through your browser settings.

Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

Since no personal data is processed, this right is not applicable.

Right to Object (Article 21)

You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests. Since we do not process personal data, this right is not applicable.

Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe that the processing of your personal data violates data protection law. See Section 8 for contact details.

6. Transfers Outside the EEA

While JW Images Search does not collect or store personal data, the following technical data transfers occur:

Google Custom Search API

When you perform a search, your query is transmitted to Google's servers via our serverless function. Google is based in the United States, and data may be transferred outside the EEA.

Google participates in standard contractual clauses and appropriate safeguards for international data transfers. For more information, see Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Vercel Hosting

This application is hosted on Vercel's infrastructure. Vercel may process certain technical data (such as IP addresses in server logs) as part of providing hosting services. Vercel is committed to GDPR compliance and uses appropriate safeguards for data transfers. For more information, see Vercel's privacy policy at https://vercel.com/privacy.

7. No Automated Decision-Making or Profiling

JW Images Search does not engage in any automated decision-making, including profiling, as defined in Article 22 of the GDPR. No decisions that affect you legally or similarly significantly are made based solely on automated processing.
